Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ):
  • Q: How many attempts are allowed for UPSC?
    A: General: 6, OBC: 9, SC/ST: Unlimited (within age limit).
  • Q: Is coaching necessary for UPSC?
    A: No, but it can help with guidance and structured preparation.
  • Q: Can I prepare for UPSC while working?
    A: Yes, with proper time management and dedication.
