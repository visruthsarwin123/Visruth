GATE Exam Preparation
Study Suggestions:
  • Understand the GATE syllabus and exam pattern thoroughly.
  • Practice previous years’ question papers and mock tests.
  • Focus on core subjects and revise regularly.
  • Join online study groups and forums for peer support.
Library & Resources:
  • Official GATE Website
  • NPTEL Online Courses

  • Practice numerical and analytical questions

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ):
  • Q: How many times can I attempt GATE?
    A: There is no age or attempt limit for GATE.
  • Q: Is a calculator allowed in the exam?
    A: Only the virtual calculator provided in the exam interface is allowed.
  • Q: Can I use my GATE score for PSU recruitment?
    A: Yes, many PSUs use GATE scores for recruitment.
